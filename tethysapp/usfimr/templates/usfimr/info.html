    App Info

    The Cohen Research App (CRA) is based off research performed by Dr. Sagy Cohen, Ph.D. at the University of Alabama.The U.S. Flood Inundation Map Repository (USFIMR) data was placed on an interactive map that includes flood location, date, affected area, and a video of the flood.


    The USFIMR project goal is to provide high-resolution inundation extent maps of U.S. floods. The flood maps were made using satellite remote sensing imagery (e.g. Landsat, Sentinel-1) with ground proofing based on secondary sources (e.g. news report, social media).


    The data used in this app was developed using shapefiles from the USFIMR website. For guidance on using the CRA please use the help guide and review the technical documentation.
